About Me

Hello! My name is Kimberley Gretchell Maydelline Sitanggang, a Computer Science - Software Engineering student at BINUS University.

I have a growing interest in UI/UX design and database development. I am developing my skills in Figma to create intuitive interface designs and using MySQL to manage and organize data.

Projects & Experiences

Christian Wijaya - Luxury Ecommerce App [2024]

A design exploration of an online store for Christian Wijaya, an Indonesian luxury brand specializing in fashion, bags, and jewelry. Built in Figma, the prototype emphasizes a sleek, elegant interface that aligns with the brand's premium identity. The project highlights a seamless shopping flow, from product browsing to checkout, while balancing visual aesthetics and usability. This work strengthened my skills in creating structured layouts, applying consistent design systems, and translating brand values into a digital platform.

Seenly - Movie Tracking & Review App [2024]

A prototype of a social platform for movie lovers, designed in Figma. Seenly allows users to search films, read and write reviews, create wishlists, mark favorites, and track watched movies.

Stylo - Outfit Planner App [2024]

A prototype of a personal outfit planning application, created in Figma. Stylo enables users to upload their clothes, mix & match outfits, and schedule what to wear on specific days using a built-in calendar. To make planning more practical, the app also integrates weather-based outfit recommendations, helping users choose suitable looks (e.g., sweaters for colder days).

"Anti Corruption" - Character Building Socialization at Tunas Bangsa Junior High School [2023]

An educational character-building activity to nurture integrity, honesty, and responsibility among Tunas Bangsa Middle School students as a step toward preventing corruption from an early stage.
